Why DRE?
The Problem
Banks take your money and earn on your deposits by funding lending operations, and the bank keeps most of what those assets earn.

When you deposit money at a traditional bank:

The bank lends it across mortgages at 6-7%, personal loans at 10-12%, and credit cards at 20-23%

You receive a fraction of a percent

The bank captures the difference

Your capital generates returns. You just never see them.

The current financial system works because opacity is built in. You can't audit where your funds go. You can't verify what they generate. You accept the rate offered because better alternatives have been out of reach.

What DRE Does Differently
DRE gives you exposure to the same asset classes banks use to generate value and DRE shares the returns with you instead of a bank's shareholders.

dreUSD is the stablecoin. dreUSDs is the savings token that earns. Converting between them is instant, with no lock-ups. Exchange to USDC instantly with a swap, or use the 6 hour express option or 7 days standard redemption for large positions.

Why This Works
Returns independent of crypto
Borrowers pay their loans whether Bitcoin is at $100,000 or $30,000. dreUSD asset backing returns are driven by real tangible assets, not token emissions or funding rates that disappear in bear markets. These assets have nearly a century of performance data and they are boring and predictable by design.

Transparent and verifiable
Every dreUSD is backed by reserves held by regulated custodians and verified through independent auditors. Third-party verifiers review the auditors' work. A public Transparency Dashboard displays reserve composition, reserve ratios, and verification status.

The verification chain is designed so that no single party, including DRE, controls the full picture. For details, see Proof of Reserves.

How DRE works
DRE introduces dreUSD, a fully backed stablecoin pegged to the US dollar. Deposit USDC to mint dreUSD.

The 2 Token Model
DRE uses 2 tokens that serve different purposes:

dreUSD is the base stablecoin pegged to $1 USD. Deposit USDC, redeem for USDC. dreUSD does not earn rewards on its own.

dreUSDs is the savings token. Convert dreUSD to dreUSDs to start earning rewards. The value of dreUSDs increases over time as rewards from the reserves are distributed to holders.

Converting between them is instant with no lock up period.

How Your Money Earns Rewards

DRE works with licensed fund managers to allocate deposited funds across three asset categories:

Property & Real Estate Credit: Secured lending against real property. Primary source of returns.

Secured Loans: Short duration loans backed by high quality collateral.

Cash Equivalents: Short term liquid instruments for fast redemptions.

These assets generate income. Borrowers make interest payments at regular intervals. Income generated by the underlying assets is distributed to dreUSDs holders, increasing value over time.

Withdrawals
Convert dreUSDs back to dreUSD instantly, then redeem dreUSD through two paths:

Express takes approximately 6 hours with a small fee.

Standard takes approximately 7 days with no fee.

When you request a redemption, you receive an NFT that tracks your claim until the request is automatically fulfilled.

Market Opportunity
The Opportunity in Property Credit
Property credit is one of the largest and most established asset classes in the world. Banks have dominated this market for decades, using depositor funds to originate mortgages and capturing the majority of returns for their shareholders. The underlying asset class has performed consistently across economic cycles, with nearly a century of data backing its resilience.

Until recently, direct access to property credit returns was limited to banks, institutional lenders, and private credit funds with high minimums. Crypto infrastructure changes that. Blockchain based settlement, transparent on-chain accounting, and programmable stablecoins make it possible to give individuals direct access to the same returns that institutions have captured for generations.

DRE starts with Australian property credit and will expand into additional markets over time. The model is designed to scale across geographies.

Why Australia First
Australia has a clear legal and regulatory framework for lending and property enforcement, creating a safe, reliable, and consistent source of revenue for dreUSDs. The land registry system is transparent and government operated, providing a verifiable chain of title and security for mortgage lenders.

Market fundamentals: Sydney and broader NSW property markets have strong demand fundamentals driven by population growth, constrained land supply in key corridors, and consistent long term price appreciation. Australia has experienced decades of continuous property value growth across major metropolitan areas.

Low natural disaster exposure: Compared to other major global markets, Sydney and the target regions carry minimal exposure to earthquakes, hurricanes, tsunamis, or major flood events. This reduces catastrophic loss risk on the underlying collateral.

Strong regulatory environment: Australia maintains a high compliance regime for construction and property development. Regulatory protections were strengthened following building quality events, and low to medium density development is particularly well regulated. DRE focuses exclusively on this segment.

Macro stability: Australia has one of the lowest government debt to GDP ratios among OECD countries, providing a stable macroeconomic backdrop for property markets and currency stability.

The Broader Vision
Australia is the starting point. DRE's property credit model is built to expand into additional markets where strong legal frameworks, transparent land registries, and established lending infrastructure exist.

Beyond property credit, DRE's reserve structure already includes secured loans and cash equivalents. Over time, the range of real world assets backing dreUSD can grow as new opportunities meet DRE's eligibility standards.
