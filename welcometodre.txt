Introducing dreUSD and dreUSDs

dreUSD is a fully backed stablecoin pegged to the US dollar, backed by real assets, and redeemable any time. Fully composable in DeFi and CeFi.

dreUSDs is the savings token that captures rewards from the assets backing dreUSD. Convert dreUSD to dreUSDs and rewards accumulate automatically. No lock-ups, no active management. Convert back instantly whenever you want.
